  Welcome to L A B O  
We've jotted some things on the whiteboard for you to try

 Search our catalogue

Try out our general purpose search interface, to get to grips with our collection
By harvesting our OAI-PMH provider we've implemented an example search engine based on ElasticSearch. We consider this search engine to be useful for exploring our collection in a general sense. If you require more specific search strategies, we've documented the current approach.

 Try out different APIs

Currently we have APIs for search, entity extraction and speech recognition
We're implementing several services that do stuff with our catalogue or can process different kinds of data. Each of these services have APIs which you can try out here. So far we have search, entity extraction and speech recogntion. We plan to have many more APIs in the near future, also exposing services for face recognition and recommendation software.

 Semantic search

Use SPARQL to search our collection semantically.
Next to ElasticSearch we've implemented a search engine based on the ClioPatria triplestore. Again we've described the import process via OAI-PMH and also how we map to well-known RDF namespaces and reconsile with our Thesaurus. We also provide a link to an RDF dump which is updated every day.

 Video recommendation

Find related news items for any news broadcast
In cooperation with the university of Amsterdam we've developed an recommendation service for eight o'clock news broadcasts.
