Contact information

To stay informed

Stay informed about the CLARIAH project and our activities via de CLARIAH agenda, nieuwspagina and nieuwsbrief. CLARIAH is also on Twitter and Facebook. Follow also the CLARIAH Media Studies blog, where media scholars write about different research topics.

Feedback & issue tracker

If you have questions about the CLARIAH Media Suite or the services for media scholars, please find us in the Gitter lobby. Anyone can use this lobby, and developers with a GitHub account can link to the GitHub issue tracker.

Contact us

For specific questions about the CLARIAH Media Studies project you can contact us via e-mail: mediastudies@clariah.nl.

For questions about the Media Suite, visit our Knowledge Base, or Use our Feedback and issue tracker pages
